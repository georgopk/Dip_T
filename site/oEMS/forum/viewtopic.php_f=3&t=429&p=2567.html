Post by thorsten » Fri 23 Dec 2016, 23:38

Hi,

the answer is quite simple. I only calculate and output the energy in the system minimum every 4 seconds (it may be longer for large simulations). Thus it can decide to finish at different timesteps, because it will not be the first TS that has the needed level, but the first I notice.
Why do it like this and not check every x time-steps? Because I do not know in advance and over time how long a single time-step is going to take and I wanted that a user gets an output on a regular (but not to often) basis...
Does this cause problems for you?

regards
Thorsten
