Post by thorsten » Wed 16 May 2018, 21:19

AddDiscMaterial just tells openEMS which file to use nothing else. So no, you do not need Matlab to only use these.
Octave actually can read and write hdf5. It just lacks the ability to write attributes or access datasets individually. openEMS itself reads/writes hdf5 with the linked C++ library of course.

Hi,

as with any other material, you need to properly debug your setup. E.g. by dumping the discretized material into an output file. See the openEMS debug options.

Possible source of error:
* Your box and discrete material domain do not match
* Your discrete material domain scale (meter vs milimeter?)
* Your mesh is to coarse to resolve the discrete material
* ...
