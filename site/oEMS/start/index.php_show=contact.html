For questions, problems, bug-reports, support, ideas etc. regarding openEMS have a look at the openEMS-Forum or write an email.

For general questions regarding numerical simulations, electromagnetic problems etc. you may have a look at the Laboratory for General and Theoretical Electrical Engineering (ATE) website.

openEMS is a project started by Thorsten Liebig at the laboratory for General and Theoretical Electrical Engineering (ATE), University of Duisburg-Essen.
